# Day Care Eggs

Eggs are server-owned `user-eggs` records rather than Pokemon records. This prevents an egg from being used in battles, teams, voyages, item targeting, or release flows before it hatches. The Pokemon Box shows eggs as normal-sized sprite tiles; opening one shows a masked `???` profile with its hatch button or countdown.

Unlocking requires the completed `day-care-egg-program` task and Researcher level 32. A successful Field Observation can find one egg with a 1-in-26 roll, provided the user's Pokemon Box capacity (Pokemon plus active eggs) is not full and they own fewer than 10 active eggs. The Field Observation's background, region, and location are retained and applied to the Pokemon when it hatches.

An egg becomes hatchable 12 hours after discovery. Hatching from the Pokemon Box costs 50 Crystals and uses a dedicated hatch reveal before the reward screen. The species is rolled only then: caught forms have 74 weight, baby forms 25, and seen-but-uncaught forms 1. Empty pools are skipped; legendary and mythical forms are never candidates. The resulting Pokemon is level 5 and grants 15 Pokemon Research XP. Eggs use a global 2x shiny multiplier.
